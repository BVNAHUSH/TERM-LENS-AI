Why is this project so important?

In an increasingly digital world, we are constantly asked to agree to terms and conditions. But who actually reads them?

Hidden Clauses

Companies often bury unfair clauses in lengthy and complex legal documents, knowing that most people won't read them.

Unexpected Costs

You could be agreeing to hidden fees, auto-renewing subscriptions, or other charges without even realizing it.

Privacy Risks

Many T&Cs grant companies broad rights to collect, use, and share your personal data in ways you might not be comfortable with.

Waiving Your Rights

You might be giving up your right to sue the company in court, forcing you into binding arbitration instead.

Understanding Subscription Traps

Many users unknowingly agree to recurring subscriptions. Reading T&Cs can save money and prevent surprises.

Empowering Users

Awareness of rights and obligations makes users more confident and responsible digital citizens.
