About Me & This Project

Hi, this is Nahush. I created this project because I believe everyone has the right to understand what they're agreeing to. Terms and conditions are often long, confusing, and filled with legal jargon that most people don't have the time or expertise to decipher. This can lead to people unknowingly giving away their rights, their data, or their money.

This project aims to solve that problem by using AI to analyze these documents and provide a clear, concise summary of the most important points. It's designed to be a tool for everyone, from the average consumer to the legal professional, to help them make more informed decisions. My goal is to make the digital world a little more transparent and fair for everyone.
